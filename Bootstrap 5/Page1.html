About Atah

At only 12 years old, Atah Abbas writes with the imagination of a wizard and the courage of a knight. His debut fantasy novel, The Soul Knight, is filled with adventure, destiny, and ancient magic.

The Soul Knight

The Soul Knight tells the story of a young warrior discovering his true powers. With enchanted forests, spirit guardians, and battles against darkness, the book brings readers into a breathtaking magical world.
